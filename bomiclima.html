Bomi Clima

Website redesign for an HVAC company, focused on clarity, product discovery, and customer inquiries.

Contact
Client:  www.bomiclima.com
Date:  2025
Role:  UX/UI Design & Web Development

Problem statement

Bomi Clima needed a clear, professional online presence to present its HVAC solutions and build trust with potential customers. The existing website lacked focus, clear structure, and strong credibility signals, making it difficult for users to understand the offering or take action.

Challenge

The challenge was organizing over 1,100 HVAC products alongside professional services into a structure users could quickly understand. The website needed to support both product discovery and service inquiries without overwhelming visitors or creating confusion between the two offerings.

Solution

We delivered a clean, structured website with simplified navigation, defined user paths, and focused calls to action. The result is a platform that presents a large product catalog clearly, positions Bomi Clima as a reliable professional partner, and supports customer inquiries and sales conversations more effectively.

How We Solved It

Clarifying Services vs. Products

We clearly separated professional HVAC services from the product catalog, ensuring users immediately understand what Bomi Clima offers and can follow the right path without hesitation.

Structuring a 1,100+ Product Catalog

Over 1,100 HVAC products were organized into a clear, logical structure that reduces cognitive load and allows users to find relevant solutions quickly and confidently.

Designing Clear User Flows

Primary user flows - product discovery, service inquiries, and information seeking - were designed with clear entry points and next steps, eliminating uncertainty and friction.

Supporting Conversion at Key Moments

Calls to action were placed strategically across the site to guide users toward contact when intent is highest, supporting inquiries without disrupting exploration.

Reinforcing Trust Through Layout and Hierarchy

A consistent layout, strong visual hierarchy, and focused content presentation were used to communicate credibility and position Bomi Clima as a reliable professional partner.

Building a Scalable Foundation

The website was designed to support ongoing growth, allowing new products, services, and content to be added without breaking clarity or structure.

Outcome

The redesigned website transformed a complex and unfocused setup into a clear, structured platform that supports both product discovery and service inquiries. Users can now navigate a large product catalog with confidence and reach key actions without friction.

The new structure reduces confusion, strengthens trust, and supports more effective sales conversations by making the offering easy to understand.

The experience was refined through iterative validation to ensure clarity across key user paths.

As a result, Bomi Clima now has a scalable, professional platform that can support future growth and evolving business needs.
