Quick guide

How does branding work with UX and web development?

+
-

We treat branding as complementary to UX and web – logos, guidelines, and visual systems integrate seamlessly with interfaces and development. Ideal when you need brand assets for a new product or redesign.

What file formats do we receive?

+
-

You receive logo files in vector (SVG, AI, EPS) and raster formats, brand guidelines as PDF, and source files in your preferred design tool for future edits.

How long does a branding project take?

+
-

Logo and colour palette: under 1 week. Digital assets or brand guidelines: 2–4 weeks. Full visual identity: 4–8 weeks. Timelines depend on scope and revision rounds.

Can you refresh our brand without changing the logo?

+
-

Yes. We can update colours, typography, and visual style while keeping your existing logo. Ideal when the mark still works but the overall brand feels outdated.
